Frequently Asked Questions

RackTrack can detect and analyze cables, switches, ports, racks, fuses, and various electrical components. Our AI models are trained on comprehensive datasets to recognize different component types, colors, and configurations commonly found in data centers.

Our AI models achieve 99.9% accuracy in component detection and classification. We continuously improve our models through ongoing training with new data and user feedback to maintain the highest possible accuracy rates.

RackTrack supports JPG, JPEG, PNG, and BMP image formats with a maximum file size of 16MB. For best results, we recommend using high-resolution images with good lighting that clearly show the rack components.

Most images are processed in under 30 seconds. Processing time may vary depending on image size, complexity, and current system load, but our optimized infrastructure ensures consistently fast results.
